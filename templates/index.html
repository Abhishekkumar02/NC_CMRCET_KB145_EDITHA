Editha OCR is a free cross-platform online Optical Character Recognition (OCR) web application that extracts text from images or scanned documents (PDF). The user can search something from the extracted text or copy it for further processing.

Text images could be a screenshot of a digital camera or photocopy of a scanned document such as books, faxes, receipts, bank statements, contracts, bills, business cards, mails, passport, and IDs.

Editha OCR recognizes multi-column documents written in 3 languages (English, Urdu, Telugu), supports several major input image formats (jpg, png) and PDF, which can be loaded from disk, displays recognized text as well as input source image side by side to facilitate reviewing mis-recognized words.
